# Toggles

You can create a toggle button with this code:

```html filename="Toggle" copy
<div class="tb-toggle" toggle-state="close">
  <div class="toggle-title">
    <h4 id="why-to-use-prime-as-your-next-theme-">
      <b>Why to use Prime as your next theme?</b>
    </h4>
    <button class="toggle-icon">
      <span class="arrow"></span>
    </button>
  </div>
  <div class="toggle-content">
    <p>Prime is a highly optimized blogger theme that comes with in-built plugins and beautiful layout. Your content shines with our theme.</p>
  </div>
</div>
```
